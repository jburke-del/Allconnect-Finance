# Glossary

One-line definitions. Follow the link for the full, authoritative entry.

- **CPA (Commission Per Active):** commission paid by the partner per activated
  unit. Higher is favorable for RV. See [kpis/cpa-total.md](kpis/cpa-total.md).
- **ACL (Average Customer Life):** expected customer lifetime in months, from the
  pooled survival curve. See [kpis/acl.md](kpis/acl.md).
- **Residual CPA:** the residual (ongoing) portion of commission per active. See
  [kpis/residual-cpa.md](kpis/residual-cpa.md).
- **M0 units:** units in a cohort at payment number zero (cohort entry).
- **PSG (Platinum Service Group):** excluded from metric calculations but
  retained in connect counts.
- **Cohort month:** the month a cohort of orders entered (grain for survival and
  residual modeling).
